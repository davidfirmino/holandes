# Official A2 vocabulary — work, school, and health

This file consolidates vocabulary from official/real A2 reading simulations in three very frequent areas: work, school/courses, and health.

## 1. Work, shops, rules, and rosters

| Dutch | English | A2 note |
|---|---|---|

| langer open zijn | to be open longer | Used when shop hours change. |
