- **Lesson 20 — De politiek:** politics, government, rights and duties

Goal: recognize A2 reading vocabulary quickly in everyday texts, notices, ads, letters, e-mails, brochures, and instructions.

---

## How to study this file

Do not try to memorize everything at once. Study in layers:

1. **Maximum priority:** words that appear often in exams and real life.
2. **Medium priority:** words useful for understanding texts.
3. **Recognition only:** specific words; just understand them in context.

Recommended method:

- Read the model sentences first.
- Then review the table.
- Translate short sentences without looking.
- Then do mini A2 texts mixing themes.

## Key explanations

### `het weer` vs `het klimaat`

- **het weer** = weather today / now / this week.
- **het klimaat** = climate of a region.

Examples:

- **Hoe is het weer vandaag?** — How is the weather today?
- **Het klimaat in Nederland is anders dan in Brazilië.** — The climate in the Netherlands is different from Brazil.

### `koud` vs `de kou`

- **koud** = cold as an adjective.
- **de kou** = the cold as a noun.

Examples:

- **Het is koud vandaag.** — It is cold today.
- **Ik hou niet van de kou.** — I do not like the cold.

### `wordt` with weather

`wordt` comes from **worden** and expresses change: to become / to get.

- **Het is koud.** — It is cold.
- **Het wordt koud.** — It is getting cold / it will become cold.
- **Morgen wordt het zonnig.** — Tomorrow it will become sunny.

## Useful phrases

- **Het regent.** — It is raining.
- **Het waait hard.** — It is very windy.
- **Het is bewolkt.** — It is cloudy.
- **Vandaag blijft het droog.** — Today it will stay dry.
- **Neem een paraplu mee.** — Take an umbrella with you.
- **In de winter vriest het soms.** — In winter it sometimes freezes.
- **De temperatuur is ongeveer 10 graden.** — The temperature is about 10 degrees.
