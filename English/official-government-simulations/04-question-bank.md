# Question bank — official-style A2 reading

Questions based on patterns from official/real A2 reading simulations. Complete them first without looking at the answer key.

## How to answer

1. Read the question.
2. Search mentally for the date, person, place, or rule.
3. Be careful with `voor`, `vanaf`, `uiterlijk`, `tot en met`, `moet`, `mag`, `hoeft niet`.
4. Only then choose A, B, or C.

## Review by error type

If you missed questions about dates, review:

- `vanaf`
- `voor / vóór`
- `uiterlijk`
- `tot en met`
- `daarna`

If you missed questions about obligation, review:

- `moeten`
- `mogen`
- `hoeven niet`
- `moeten wel`
- `maximaal`
- `minimaal`

If you missed questions about services, review:

- `openingstijden`
- `afspraak maken`
- `spoedafspraak`
- `receptie`
- `klantenservice`
- `informatiebalie`

If you missed questions about work, review:

- `werkrooster`
- `dienst`
- `leidinggevende`
- `afdeling`
- `balie`
- `extra werken`
- `vakantie opnemen`
