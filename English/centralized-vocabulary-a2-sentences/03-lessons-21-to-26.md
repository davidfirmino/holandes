# Lessons 21 to 26 — Centralized vocabulary with A2 sentences

Format: **Dutch word + English translation + A2 sentence + sentence translation**.

> Note: this block works as a practical consolidation for the end of A2. It uses vocabulary typical of communication, forms, services, money, practical health, work, and exams.
