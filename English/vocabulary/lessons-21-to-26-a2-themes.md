# Vocabulary A2 — Lessons 21 to 26

This file consolidates the final thematic lessons from the book, with a focus on **A2 reading**:

- **Lesson 21 — De computer:** computer, internet, e-mail, files, and digital actions
- **Lesson 22 — Een woning zoeken:** looking for housing, renting, buying, renovation
- **Lesson 23 — Het onderwijs:** education, courses, studying, exams
- **Lesson 24 — Beroep en werk:** profession, work, salary, contract, job vacancy
- **Lesson 25 — De economie:** bank, money, company, costs, profit, debt
- **Lesson 26 — De gezondheid:** health, doctor, medicine, hospital, insurance

Goal: quickly recognize words that appear in A2 exam texts, especially e-mails, ads, letters, official messages, forms, and instructions.

---

# How to study these lessons

For A2 reading, do not try to speak everything perfectly. The focus is recognizing patterns:

- who writes;
- who receives the message;
- what the subject is;
- what the person needs to do;
- deadline/date/time;
- what is required, forbidden, allowed, or optional.

When reviewing, always ask:

1. Does this word appear in a letter, ad, website, or form?
2. Does it show an action? Example: `inloggen`, `verhuizen`, `aanmelden`, `betalen`.
3. Does it show a rule? Example: `verplicht`, `niet toegestaan`, `moet`.
4. Does it show money? Example: `huurprijs`, `factuur`, `kosten`, `schuld`.
5. Does it show health or urgency? Example: `huisarts`, `recept`, `ambulance`, `EHBO`.
